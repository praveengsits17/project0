First Time : Away from Home

Staying with family from childhood and suddenly you have to move for internship to other state, new people, different language, no friends, is tough to handle in beginning. You start thinking better to run away from the place and go back to home. If at that time you run away you are never going to learn to stay alone, or be independent. At that time same happened with me, I want to return back but my mentors explained me, and said just stay for 15 days if you don't like come back home, I stayed there for 15 days and I liked the work place and my colleagues also supported me. This was the best experience I had till this date.
